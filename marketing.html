LineVision – See the Line Before You Go

LineVision helps you avoid long waits by showing real-time crowd estimates outside participating bars. Using AI-based people counting and periodic image snapshots from camera-equipped venues, LineVision lets users make smarter decisions for their night out.

This app is launching with example/demo bars. As more bars sign on, real live images and wait estimates will appear in-app. We're actively expanding in Madison and the Twin Cities.
