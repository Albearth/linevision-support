LineVision

Know the line before you go.

LineVision is a real-time crowd tracking app that helps you see how long the line is outside your favorite bars. Using camera snapshots and AI-powered people counting, LineVision gives you a live view of the wait — so you can make better decisions for your night out.

The app is launching with demo venues to showcase how the system works. As more bars come online, real live images and crowd counts will appear in the app. We’re actively partnering with venues in Madison and the Twin Cities!
